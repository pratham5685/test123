Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our programs and approach.

What age groups do you teach?

Our courses are tailored for students in Grades 4-9 (approximately ages 6-14), with curriculum carefully designed for each developmental stage.

Is prior coding experience required?

Not at all! Our programs are designed to engage complete beginners, with personalized instruction adapting to each child's starting point.

How long are the sessions?

Each 1-on-1 session lasts 60 minutes, providing focused instruction without overwhelming younger attention spans.

What if my child doesn't enjoy the class?

We offer a no-obligation free trial session. If your child doesn't enjoy it, there's no commitment to continue.
